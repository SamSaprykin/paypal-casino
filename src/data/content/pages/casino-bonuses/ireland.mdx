      ## Wagering Requirements: The Number That Really Matters

      Wagering requirements (also called "playthrough" or "rollover") are how many times you must bet a bonus before you can withdraw any winnings from it. This single number decides whether a bonus is generous or nearly impossible to clear.

      If you claim a €100 bonus with 35x wagering, you must place €3,500 in bets before the bonus (and its winnings) becomes withdrawable. Here's how the same €100 bonus scales at different requirements:

      | Wagering | Total to bet on €100 bonus | Realistic verdict |
      |---|---|---|
      | 20x | €2,000 | Excellent – very clearable |
      | 30x | €3,000 | Good – the fair-market standard |
      | 40x | €4,000 | Average – manageable with time |
      | 60x+ | €6,000+ | Steep – often not worth it |

      <div class="info-card">
      <h4>Other terms that change a bonus's real value</h4>
      Max bet while wagering (often €5), game weighting (slots usually count 100%, table games far less), time limits (7–30 days), and max cashout on winnings from no-deposit offers. A "smaller" bonus with fair terms usually beats a huge one with hostile ones.
      </div>

      ## Free Spins and No Deposit Bonuses

      Free spins are one of the most popular bonus types because they're simple and fun: a fixed number of spins on a specific slot, with any winnings usually paid as bonus funds subject to wagering. The two numbers that matter are the **spin value** (a 100-spin offer at €0.10 is worth €10, not a fortune) and any **cap on winnings**.

      No deposit bonuses – a small amount of cash or spins just for signing up – are the only truly risk-free offers, which is exactly why their terms are the strictest. Expect low winnings caps (often €50–€100), high wagering, and tight time limits. They're perfect for test-driving a casino, not for chasing big wins.

      <div class="tip-box">
      **Pro tip:** With free spins, check which slot they're locked to. Spins on a high-RTP, low-volatility slot give you far more playtime and a better shot at clearing wagering than spins forced onto a single high-variance game.

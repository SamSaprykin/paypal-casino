      ## Bonuses at New Casinos: Bigger Isn't Always Better

      New casinos lead with their bonuses because it's the fastest way to attract players. You'll often see larger headline figures than at established brands – but the value is in the terms, not the number. A "200% up to €1,000" offer with 60x wagering is worth far less than "100% up to €200" with 30x.

      | Bonus type | Typical at new casinos | What to check |
      |---|---|---|
      | Welcome match | 100%–200% up to €500–€1,000 | Wagering requirement and max bet while active |
      | Free spins | 50–200 spins, often on one slot | Spin value and any winnings cap |
      | No deposit | Small (€5–€10 or 20–50 spins) | Heavily capped winnings; read the fine print |
      | Reload / cashback | Weekly reloads, 5–15% cashback | Whether it's wager-free (cashback often is) |

      <div class="tip-box">
      **Pro tip:** Before opting in, divide the bonus amount by the wagering multiple. A €500 bonus at 40x means €20,000 in turnover to clear it. If that number feels unrealistic for your budget, decline the bonus and play with your cash instead – many new casinos let you opt out.
